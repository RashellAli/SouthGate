Privacy Policy

At SouthGate Protection, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any personal information you provide when you contact us through our website.

Information We Collect

When you use the contact form on our website, we collect your name, email address, and the message you send to us. This information is necessary for us to respond to your enquiries effectively.

How We Use Your Information

The personal data you provide is used solely to communicate with you and respond to your questions or requests. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing or any other purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use FormSubmit to handle contact form submissions. FormSubmit processes your information only to forward it to our email address. You can learn more about their privacy practices on their website at https://formsubmit.co/privacy.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights or have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at info@southgateprotection.co.uk.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
