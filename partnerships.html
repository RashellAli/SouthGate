Partnerships

SouthGate Protection CIC collaborates with trusted partners to strengthen community safety and protect vulnerable residents.

Faron Paul runs FazAmnesty, a weapons amnesty initiative dedicated to reducing knife crime and promoting safer communities. Our partnership allows us to support knife collections and community safety patrols together.

I had the privilege of working alongside Faron Paul during the Notting Hill Carnival, supporting knife amnesty collections and community protection efforts. This partnership demonstrates how combined efforts can make a real difference.
