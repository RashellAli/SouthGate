Partnerships

SouthGate Protection collaborates with trusted partners to strengthen community safety and effectively support each other’s initiatives.

I was honoured to work alongside Faron Paul at the Notting Hill Carnival. As a team, we supported medics in navigating the busy crowd, assisted individuals who were unwell or injured, and actively participated in safe and responsible weapon collections, contributing to a safer and more secure environment for all attendees.
