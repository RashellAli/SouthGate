Partnerships

Working with trusted partners, SouthGate Protection strengthens community safety, supports knife crime prevention and local programs across Croydon.

Faron Paul | FazAmnesty ▾

SouthGate Protection CIC was proud to work alongside Faron Paul and his team at the Notting Hill Carnival. Together, we ensured public safety by assisting medics through the busy crowd, helping those who were passed out or injured, and supported weapon collections, resulting in 14 weapon surrenders over the carnival weekend.
