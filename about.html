About SouthGate Protection CIC

Dedicated to protecting our community, SouthGate Protection CIC works to ensure safety, visibility, and trust across Croydon.

Our Mission

At SouthGate Protection CIC, our mission is simple: safety, visibility, and community trust. We patrol local streets to protect all residents, while providing extra care and support to groups such as women, children, and the elderly. Every action we take is guided by integrity, professionalism, and compassion.

Our Story

Founded by Rashell Ali Chowdhury, SouthGate Protection CIC was created to address gaps in community safety and support for those who may need extra help. With a focus on proactive patrols and partnerships with local organizations, we aim to make Croydon a safer place for everyone to live, work, and thrive.

What We Do

  • Community Patrols: Regular and visible patrols to deter crime and reassure residents.
  • Partnerships & Collaboration: Working with organizations like Faron Paul/FazAmnesty to raise awareness and run initiatives such as knife amnesties.
  • Education & Awareness: Providing guidance and resources to the public on personal safety and crime prevention.

Our Impact

  • Assisted hundreds of residents across Croydon.
  • Supported multiple events and knife amnesty collections, including the Notting Hill Carnival, resulting in 14 weapons surrendered in one weekend.
  • Built strong relationships with local organizations and the community, reinforcing trust and visibility.

Why We Do It

We believe everyone deserves to feel safe in their community. By combining professional patrols, community engagement, and strategic partnerships, we aim to create safer streets and stronger neighborhoods, while giving extra support to those who need it most.
